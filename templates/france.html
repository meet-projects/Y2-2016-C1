Some General Knowledge

France is a sovereign state comprising territory in western Europe and several overseas regions and territories French is the dominant language of the country's 66 million residents.
French is the second most widely learned foreign language in the world. The French embody romance and passion and believe in égalité, which means equality, and is part of the country’s motto.

Food

Foie Gras is one of the most popular and well-known delicacies in Frenchcuisine and its flavour is described as rich, buttery, and delicat.

Clafouti is possibly the most famous traditional dessert from the south west of France. 'Le Clafouti' is traditionally made with wild cherries and comes from Limousin.

Cassoulet is in my opinion one of the best traditional country dishes from France. Hearty and delicious, made well it warms the soul.

Places To Visit

the most visited place in Paris is the Eiffel Tower and Disneyland Paris.
At the same time, the famed cathedral of Notre Dame is popular with both new and veteran visitors to this City of Lights. Thus, Paris is often viewed by
visitors as the top city to visit.

If you want to be fully engrossed in the natural beauty of France, then a must see place is the famed city that is the Mont Saint-Michel Abbey in the Normandy region.
There are plenty of great restaurants, stores and places to climb and enjoy dynamic views of the landscape and ocean that surround Mont Saint-Michel.

The French people call the Palace of Versailles “Chateau de Versailles,” it's a stunningly beautiful location with a local city that’s also breathtaking.
The city is an easy 40-minute train ride from downtown Paris.
