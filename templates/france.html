France

France is a sovereign state comprising territory in western Europe
and several overseas regions and territories French is the dominant language of the country's 66 million residents.
French is the second most widely learned foreign language in the world. The French embody romance and passion and believe in égalité, which means
equality, and is part of the country’s motto.



Foie Gras is one of the most popular and well-known delicacies
in Frenchcuisine and its flavour is described as rich, buttery,
and delicat.





Clafouti is possibly the most famous traditional dessert from the
south west of France. 'Le Clafouti' is traditionally made with wild
cherries and comes from Limousin.





Cassoulet is in my opinion one of the best traditional country dishes
from France. Hearty and delicious, made well it warms the soul.
