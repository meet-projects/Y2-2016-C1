Some General Knowledge

Peru is a country in western South America.
it's a distillation of the oldest traditions and the finest building, weaving and art made by the most sophisticated cultures on the continent.
In Peru the wildest landscapes – from frozen Andean peaks to the deep Amazon – help us re-forge our connection to the natural world.

Food

"My first encounter with comida Peruana was over 20 years ago, thanks to my wife's Peruvian family. Their cooking was a study in juxtaposition: hot and cold, acidic and starchy, robust and delicate"

Almost as popular as ceviche, this chifa dish represents a fusion of Chinese stir frying and classic Peruvian ingredients.

Places To Visit

Puno is a port city on the shores of Lake Titicaca on the Altiplano of Peru.
Despite its picturesque hillside setting, the city itself is a ramshackle collection of mostly unfinished modern buildings.

Once just an attractive roadside fishing port, Mancora is now the most trendy beach in Peru, attracting a surf crowd from around the globe.
It’s a highly welcome and very enjoyable stopover when traveling along the north coast.
