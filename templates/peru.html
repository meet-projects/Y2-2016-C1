Peru is a country in western South America.
it's a distillation of the oldest traditions and the finest building,
weaving and art made by the most sophisticated cultures on the continent.
In Peru the wildest landscapes – from frozen Andean peaks to the deep Amazon –
help us re-forge our connection to the natural world.

Puno is a port city on the shores of Lake Titicaca on the Altiplano of Peru.
Despite its picturesque hillside setting, the city itself is a ramshackle collection
of mostly unfinished modern buildings.






Once just an attractive roadside fishing port, Mancora is now the most
trendy beach in Peru, attracting a surf crowd from around the globe.
It’s a highly welcome and very enjoyable stopover when traveling along the north coast.
